mun o, wawa e mi kin  
mi toki e ni

sina wile ala wile tawa musi  
lon nasin kalama  
lon poka mi

mi tawa insa pi supa kalama  
kulupu li mu, li open e musi ona

o lon e kalama  
o sitelen e ale ma  
o sijelo e olin mi
