suno li lon  
o lukin e sewi  
o pilin e kon  
jan ale li sona  
e wile suwi  
o kute e kalama ni!

poki suno lili o!  
o kalama e olin sina  
tawa ale lon ma ale  
pilin suno o lon pona!  
wasoweli suwi o!  
o pana sin e suno sina  
mi o sona e lon ona  
olin lon la jan li wawa!
