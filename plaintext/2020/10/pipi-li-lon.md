sewi pimeja lon anpa  \
mun lili walo lon poka  \
pipi li kon, pipi li kon  \
sina wile e seme

mun lon sewi li telo  \
kasi lon anpa li moli  \
kon lete, kon lete  \
lon lon poka sina

pipi kon, kasi lape  \
tu li pona tawa ale  \
pimeja li ike ala  \
apeja li ike wawa  \
lape anu seme  \
ni anu nasin ante
